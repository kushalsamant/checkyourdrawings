## What this tool does

Visual comparison for two drawing PDFs.

- **Drawing A** (earlier) and **Drawing B** (later)
- PDF plots or exports, up to **100 MB** each
- Outputs **PNG** and **PDF** overlays
- **Not AI** — aligns and compares geometry only
- **Assists review** — not a substitute for QA sign-off
- Does not guarantee every change is found

---

## How it works

1. Upload two PDF revisions
2. Sheets align automatically; overlay builds in minutes
3. Download **PNG** or **PDF**; review using your normal process

### Overlay colors

Blue — Additions (only in Drawing B)  
Orange — Removals (only in Drawing A)  
Green — Matching geometry (both revisions)  
Red — Misaligned overlap (inspect before deciding)

---

## Why alignment matters

Sheets slightly out of register hide real differences.

Red highlights areas to inspect first — not pass or fail.

Built by an architect who manually aligned revisions for years.
