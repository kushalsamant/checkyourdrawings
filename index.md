## The problem

Drawing revisions easily hide crucial changes.
A new PDF can look almost identical to the previous issue.
Lines shift by mere millimeters, and title blocks get reformatted.
Critical differences remain invisible at a glance.
As a result, the review process often begins before anyone even spots the drift.

---

## How teams work today

Today, teams are forced to align sheets and compare them by eye.
You endlessly pan and zoom across two PDFs side by side.
You overlay plots on a screen or hold paper up to the light.
You hunt manually for moved dimensions and shifted walls.
Large drawing sets eat up hours of valuable time each cycle.
Ultimately, the entire process relies heavily on a single careful reviewer.

---

## Why that is error-prone

Sheets that are slightly out of register mask actual differences.
A slightly shifted grid makes perfectly matching geometry look like a major change.
Conversely, a real, critical edit can hide behind a near-overlap.
Manual alignment simply does not scale reliably across different design disciplines.
This tool was built to solve this exact frustration by an architect who spent years aligning revisions.

---

## What Check Your Drawings does

Check Your Drawings precisely aligns your two revision PDFs.
Simply upload Drawing A and Drawing B.
The service registers the sheets automatically behind the scenes.
It runs a pure geometry comparison—relying on hard data, not AI interpretation.
You receive clean PNG and PDF overlay outputs.
From there, you proceed with your standard QA review process.

---

## The benefit

All changes stand out immediately in a color-coded overlay.
Additions, removals, and exact matches can be read in seconds.
Red highlights instantly show where misalignments need a closer look.
The tedious guesswork of side-by-side comparison drops out of the loop entirely.
The overlay is designed to support your professional judgment, not replace it.
You retain full control over deciding what each highlight means.

---

## What it does

Provides accurate visual comparison for two drawing PDFs.
Drawing A acts as your earlier version, and Drawing B is the later revision.
It accepts PDF plots or exports up to 100 MB each.
It quickly outputs PNG and PDF overlays ready for download.
The system strictly aligns and compares raw geometry.
It acts as an assistant to your review, never as a substitute for official sign-off.

---

## What it does not do

Does not guarantee every single microscopic change is flagged.
Does not replace your existing CAD software or QA workflow.
Does not read or extract title blocks as semantic data.
Does not issue automatic pass or fail judgments on your drawings.
Does not store your confidential files beyond approximately 24 hours.
Does not substitute for thorough professional review.

---

## How it works

1. Upload two PDF revisions
2. Sheets align automatically; the overlay builds in minutes
3. Download **PNG** or **PDF**; review using your normal process

### Overlay colors

Blue — Additions (only in Drawing B)  
Orange — Removals (only in Drawing A)  
Green — Matching geometry (both revisions)  
Red — Misaligned overlap (inspect before deciding)
