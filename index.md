# Check Your Drawings
**Stop squinting at PDFs. Spot revision changes instantly.**

Drawing revisions hide their changes. Lines shift by millimeters, notes get reformatted, and a new PDF can look almost identical to the last. Review often starts before anyone spots the drift. 

Built by an architect who aligned revisions for years, **Check Your Drawings** automatically aligns and compares your PDFs so you can stop guessing and start reviewing.

---

## The Old Way is Broken
Today, you likely align sheets and compare them by eye. You pan, zoom, and overlay plots side-by-side, hunting for moved dimensions and shifted walls. 

This process eats hours and is highly error-prone:
* **False changes:** Sheets slightly out of register or a shifted grid make matching geometry look changed.
* **Hidden edits:** Real, critical edits can easily hide behind near-overlaps.
* **Bottlenecks:** The entire process relies heavily on one careful reviewer, and manual alignment simply does not scale across disciplines.

---

## How Check Your Drawings Fixes It
Upload Drawing A (earlier) and Drawing B (later). The service automatically registers the sheets and runs a pure geometry comparison—no AI interpretation, just hard data. 

Guesswork drops out of the loop. Changes show up in a color-coded overlay, allowing you to easily spot additions, removals, and matches in seconds. **The overlay supports your judgment; it doesn't replace it.**

### How it works
1. **Upload** your two PDF revisions (up to 100 MB each).
2. **Wait minutes, not hours** as sheets align automatically and the overlay builds.
3. **Download** a **PNG** or **PDF** overlay and review using your normal QA process.

> ### The Color Key
> * 🔵 **Blue:** Additions (only in Drawing B)
> * 🟠 **Orange:** Removals (only in Drawing A)
> * 🟢 **Green:** Matching geometry (both revisions)
> * 🔴 **Red:** Misaligned overlap (requires your inspection)
